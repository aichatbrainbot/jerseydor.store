# Neon Kicks Brand System

## Brand Position

Premium football streetwear with a dark luxury, editorial, tech-forward feel. The store should feel closer to a fashion label than a commodity ecommerce catalog.

## Color System

- **Ink Black:** primary canvas for every page.
- **Graphite:** elevated panels, nav, and product metadata.
- **Electric Blue:** primary action, focus states, active links, editorial dividers.
- **Soft Purple:** secondary accent for badges, subtle borders, and controlled glow.
- **Bone White:** high-contrast typography.
- **Steel Gray:** supporting copy and quiet metadata.

Use accents sparingly. Avoid large rainbow gradients and loud neon fills.

## Typography

- **Inter:** body text and UI labels.
- **Outfit:** primary headings and product names.
- **Space Grotesk:** prices, tags, badges, and short technical details.

Headlines should be confident and clean with normal letter spacing. Use uppercase only for short labels, not long paragraphs.

## Spacing

- Mobile sections: 64-80px vertical rhythm.
- Desktop sections: 96-128px vertical rhythm.
- Product grids: generous gutters with clear image dominance.
- Panels: compact internal padding, never crowded.

## Components

- **Cards:** 8px radius, thin graphite borders, black surfaces, image-first composition.
- **Buttons:** minimal, high contrast, subtle lift/glow on hover.
- **Inputs:** dark fields, calm borders, electric focus ring.
- **Badges:** small, uppercase, Space Grotesk, low glow.

## Motion

Use slow opacity/translate transitions and image scale on hover. Avoid flashy animation loops. Respect reduced-motion preferences.

## Imagery

Use large cinematic crops, editorial layouts, and Pinterest-friendly tall product frames. Product visuals should feel inspectable, not stock-background decoration.
